# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 10, 2026 (v1.1)

This Privacy Policy explains how ShapeYou ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Workout Planner app ("App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign in with Google (via Firebase Authentication) we receive:

- Your **email address**
- Your **display name** (if provided by Google)

### Fitness Questionnaire Data

When you complete the onboarding questionnaire or update your profile, we collect:

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Full name | |
| Age | |
| Gender | |
| Height (cm) | |
| Weight (kg) | |
| Fitness goals | e.g. muscle gain, fat loss, endurance |
| Current fitness level | |
| Training history | |
| Training split preference | |
| Workout days per week | |
| Workout location | e.g. gym, home |
| Session duration (minutes) | |
| Preferred workout types | e.g. strength, cardio |
| Available equipment | |
| Injuries & limitations | Free-text field — **health-related data** |

The "Injuries & limitations" field may contain sensitive health information. We use it only to tailor workout recommendations and do not share it beyond what is described in this policy.

### Usage & Technical Data

- Workout generation requests and outcomes (e.g. generation status)
- App version, device OS (collected automatically by Firebase)
- Crash reports (collected via Firebase Crashlytics, if enabled)

### Subscription & Billing Data

Subscription transactions are handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive only your **subscription entitlement status** (free / premium) from RevenueCat; we never see or store your payment card details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data used |
|---------|-----------|
| Generate personalised workout plans | Questionnaire data (sent to AI service) |

## 3. Third-Party Processors

We share your data only with the following processors as necessary to provide the service:

| Processor | Purpose | Privacy reference |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Google Firebase** | Authentication, database, crash reporting | [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management | [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy) |
| **AI workout generation service** | Generating your workout plan from questionnaire data | Provider disclosed on request |
| **Apple / Google** | App distribution & in-app billing | Subject to their platform policies |

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

---

## 4. Data Retention

- **Active accounts.** We retain your data while your account is active.
- **Account deletion.** When you delete your account (via the App), we delete your profile and questionnaire data within 30 days. Anonymised aggregate analytics may be retained longer.
- **Legal holds.** We may retain data longer if required by law.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data (editable from the Profile screen)
- **Delete** your data (delete your account in the App)
- **Port** your data (contact us for an export)
- **Object** to or **restrict** processing (contact us)

To exercise any right, email **support@shapeyou.app**. We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a person under 18 has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (HTTPS, Firebase security rules, access controls) to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
